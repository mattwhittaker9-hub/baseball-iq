01

Who We Are

Baseball IQ is an educational baseball training application available at playbaseballiq.com. We are an independent app and operate as its creator. If you have questions about this policy, see Section 10 for how to reach us.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Baseball IQ web application, including players, coaches, and parents who create accounts on behalf of minors.

03

How We Use It

Your data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Tracking your scores, IQ rating, and progress over time
  • Displaying your username on leaderboards and team rosters
  • Personalizing questions based on your position preference
  • Allowing coaches to view their team's training progress
  • Sending password reset emails (only when you request one)
  • Investigating reported questions to improve content quality
  • Diagnosing bugs and improving app performance
We will never: send you marketing emails, show you ads, sell your data, or use your information for any purpose unrelated to operating Baseball IQ.

05

Children & COPPA

⚾
Baseball IQ is designed for players of all ages, including children under 13. We take the privacy of young players seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

For users under 13 years old:

  • Parental consent is required before creating an account. By signing a child up, a parent or guardian confirms they have reviewed this policy and consent to data collection on their child's behalf.
  • We collect only the minimum information necessary — a username, email, age group, and gameplay data.
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information from children.
  • We do not use children's data for advertising or share it with third parties for marketing.
  • We recommend not using your child's real full name as their username on the leaderboard.
  • Parents or guardians may request to review, correct, or delete their child's data at any time by contacting us (see Section 10).
For coaches: When you add players to your team, you are responsible for having obtained appropriate consent from those players and their parents. Do not add players who have not agreed to participate.
06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request we fix inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request we delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email in Section 10. We will respond within 30 days. Account deletion removes your profile, scores, and leaderboard entries.

If you are located in the European Union or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA respectively. We honor these rights for all users regardless of location.

07

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — authentication is handled by Supabase with industry-standard hashing
  • Database access requires authenticated API keys not exposed publicly
  • We do not store payment information (Baseball IQ is free)

No internet transmission or storage system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

08

Cookies & Local Storage

Baseball IQ uses browser local storage (not traditional tracking cookies) to:

  • Keep you signed in between sessions
  • Remember your onboarding status
  • Cache your profile locally for faster loading
  • Store your position preference and game progress temporarily

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking cookies, or third-party analytics that profile you across the web. You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings — this signs you out but does not delete your account.

09

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and notify you by email or in-app notice for material changes.

Your continued use of Baseball IQ after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. If you disagree with changes, you can request account deletion at any time.
